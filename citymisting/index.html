Harvards CS50X

CS50X is a online course provided by Harvard that is a free intro course into computer science. This course is amazingly structured and taught, and it has guided me and helped me develop a passion for the field. I believe if anyone has a slight intrest in the subject to just look up there videos on youtube and give them a shot. The energy of the main teacher, David J. Malan, was captivating and helped motivate me to learn more and continue on in the course.

Bootstrap

I built my first website with vanillia HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and I developed an understaning of the complexity of website design and how dificult it can be to get your idea on the page when starting from the ground up. Bootstrap is a CSS frame work, which essentially is premade styles for the elements of your webpage. This tool and tools like it allow you to build website much more efficiently. Dont re-invent the wheel if you dont have to.

Independent Research

Independent research has become a major part of my progression. Either due to a problem I am unable to figure out or just simple curiosity. Self teaching is one of the greatest abilities one can have especially durring the age of the internet. Not to say it is the best form of learning, I believe that is far from true. But there will not always be someone to teach you what you want or to the standard you need. Being either YouTube videos or reading the documentation of what you are using. Learning to find answers on my own has been one of the most usefull skills I have developed.
